UFO Sightings

Analyzing reported UFO sightings across time and space

By: Nick Hafer and Miranda Liu

For our final project, we examined a dataset of over 80,000 reported Unidentified Flying Object (UFO) sightings from 1906 to 2014. We are both interested in astronomy and thought it would be valuable to look more in-depth into people's experiences with UFOs. Most existing reporting and analysis of UFO sightings is localized to certain regions or countries, but the dataset we examined is cross-national and continental, albeit with most of the reported sightings in the United States.
First, we looked at the geographic breakdown of UFO sightings. Most of the reports are from the United States, with Europe in second place. Most of the reports within the United States are on the East coast.
